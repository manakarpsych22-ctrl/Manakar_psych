Hi, I'm Sayli — and I'm Here to Help You Heal

I'm Sayli A. Kharche, a licensed Clinical Psychologist based in Pune with over 5 years of experience in mental health care. I hold an M.Phil in Clinical Psychology and am registered with the Rehabilitation Council of India (RCI).

Over the years, I've had the privilege of working with people from all walks of life — children struggling with learning difficulties, teenagers battling anxiety, adults dealing with depression and burnout, couples trying to rebuild trust, and families learning to communicate better.

My approach is simple: I listen without judgment, I understand with empathy, and I work with you to find practical solutions that actually fit your life. Whether it's through Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), mindfulness-based techniques, or supportive counseling — I tailor every session to what YOU need.

I started Manakar with a belief that mental health care should feel safe, approachable, and human. You don't need to have a "big problem" to seek help. Sometimes, just having someone to talk to can change everything.

If you've been thinking about therapy but aren't sure — take that first step. I promise, it's easier than you think.

How Can I Help You?

Every journey is unique. Explore the services tailored to support your well-being.

Individual Therapy

Personalized one-on-one sessions to help you work through anxiety, depression, stress, trauma, and other personal challenges at your own pace.

Child & Adolescent Therapy

Specialized support for children and teenagers dealing with behavioral issues, learning difficulties, emotional challenges, exam stress, and developmental concerns in a safe, playful environment.

Couple & Family Therapy

Strengthen your relationships through guided conversations that improve communication, rebuild trust, resolve conflicts, and bring families closer together.

Cognitive Retraining

Structured exercises and techniques designed to improve memory, attention, problem-solving, and other thinking skills affected by injury, illness, or mental health conditions.

Supportive Therapy

A safe, empathetic space where you can express your thoughts and feelings freely. Supportive therapy helps you cope with life's challenges, build emotional strength, and feel heard.

Corporate Wellness

Workshops, stress management programs, and employee wellness initiatives designed to foster a healthier, more productive workplace culture.

Online Sessions

Convenient and confidential virtual therapy sessions from the comfort of your home. Available across India via secure video platforms.

Psychological Assessments

Comprehensive psychological testing including IQ assessment, personality profiling, career aptitude, autism spectrum evaluation, neuropsychological assessment, learning disability screening, and clinical diagnosis — all using standardized tools with detailed written reports provided.

🔒 Privacy Policy — Manakar

Effective: March 2026 | Compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA), 2023

1. Data We Collect

When you submit the appointment form, we collect: Name, Phone Number, Email Address, Preferred Service, Date & Time, and your Message. The self-check quiz is completely anonymous — no data is stored.

2. Purpose of Collection

Your data is used only to: (a) schedule & confirm your appointment, (b) send you an email confirmation, (c) add the appointment to the clinic calendar, and (d) contact you regarding your booking.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in a private, password-protected Google Workspace account accessible only to Ms. Sayli A. Kharche (Clinical Psychologist & Clinic Administrator). Data is transmitted over HTTPS encryption. No data is stored on the website itself.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We never sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third party. Your information stays strictly between you and Manakar.

5. Your Rights (DPDPA 2023)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, you have the right to:
✓ Access — Request a copy of your stored data
✓ Correction — Request correction of inaccurate data
✓ Erasure — Request complete deletion of your data
✓ Withdraw Consent — Revoke consent at any time
To exercise these rights, email manakarpsych22@gmail.com or call +91 97308 77626.

6. Cookies & Tracking

This website uses Google reCAPTCHA for spam protection (governed by Google's Privacy Policy). We use localStorage solely to prevent duplicate form submissions. No analytics cookies are used unless explicitly stated.

7. Data Retention

Appointment records are retained for 3 years for clinical reference, after which they are permanently deleted unless required by Indian law.
